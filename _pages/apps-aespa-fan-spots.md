aespa Fan Spots is an unofficial fan-made travel guide app for overseas MYs and aespa fans visiting Korea.

The app helps users find aespa-related fan spots, cafes, restaurants, popup areas, goods shops, filming places, photo spots, and travel stops in a simple mobile guide.

This app does not require account registration or login. Saved spots are stored only on the user's device.

## Main Features

- Explore aespa-related fan spots in Korea
- Search by place, area, tag, or member name
- Browse spots related to Karina, Winter, Giselle, and Ningning
- View cafes, restaurants, popup areas, goods shops, filming places, and photo spots
- Save favorite spots on the device
- Open places directly in Google Maps
- Suggest a new spot by email
- Choose English or Korean on first launch

## How to Use

1. Select English or Korean when opening the app for the first time.
2. Browse aespa-related spots from the main screen.
3. Search by place name, area, tag, or member name.
4. Save favorite spots on your device.
5. Open a place in Google Maps when you want to visit.
6. Use the Suggest a Spot option to send a new place suggestion by email.
